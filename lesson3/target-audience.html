Target Audience:

The target audience is both male and female from different age groups who lives in this area or who are traveling to this area.

Persona 1.


Aaron B. who is 34 years old with a Bachelors in Web Design who works for Facebook. He is using the site because he is planning a trip with his family and needs to be prepared for the weather because they want to spend time outdoors with their relatives to visit different sights. He can access it through different devices.

Persona 2.


Camila H. who is 22 years old student living in Saratoga Springs. She is using this site because she plan her birthday party outside an want to make sure that everything will be ok. She can access it from different devices but prefer to use her mobile phone.

Persona 3.


Greg and Shirley M. who are both 70 years old and retired from job. They lives in this area and using this site to plan activity for their family reunion and provide information for their family members. They are not familiar with accessing to internet through mobile phone but can do it through their personal iPads.
